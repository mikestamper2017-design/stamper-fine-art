The Artist

Mike Stamper is a Canadian multi-disciplinary artist whose work explores the intersection of fluid movement and structural form. With a career spanning decades, his pieces are held in private and corporate collections globally.

As a stone sculptor, painter, woodworker, and published poet, Stamper’s work is a dialogue between the physical and the lyrical. An avid surfer and foiler, he translates the raw energy of the elements into the minimalist lines of his ink studies and the tactile permanence of his sculpture.

His work is a reflection of a life lived outdoors, capturing the rugged geometry of the Canadian landscape and the constant motion of the water.
