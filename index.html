The Artist

Mike Stamper is a Canadian multi-disciplinary artist whose work explores movement and form through various mediums including hand-carved soapstone, watercolour, and acrylics. His process is deeply rooted in the tactile nature of his materials, allowing the unique characteristics of each stone or brushstroke to guide the final expression.

Drawing inspiration from the rugged Canadian landscape and the fluidity of natural forms, Stamper's work captures a sense of quiet strength and organic grace. His sculptures often reveal the hidden spirit within the stone, while his paintings use light and texture to evoke the atmosphere of the wild.

With a career spanning several decades, his pieces are held in private collections across North America. He continues to push the boundaries of his craft from his studio, seeking new ways to translate the beauty of the natural world into enduring works of art.
